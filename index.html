About Us

We provide in person play-based sessions to infants and toddlers (0-3) with developmental disabilities, developmental delays or who are considered at risk. We address the following developmental areas in our one-on-one sessions: Cognitive, Language, Fine and Gross Motor, Social Emotional and Self-Help skills in the most enjoyable process. We use the Hawaii Early Learning Profile (HELP). Our curriculum is comprehensive, family-centered and it’s an going assessment process for infants and toddlers with developmental disabilities or developmental delays. Service areas that we cover for in-home sessions: Azusa, Baldwin Park, City of Industry, Covina, Diamond Bar, Duarte, El Monte, South El Monte, Glendora, Hacienda Heights, La Puente, Monrovia, Pomona, Rowland Heights, Walnut and West Covina.

Meet the Team

Our early intervention specialists obtain bachelor’s degrees in child development, Early Childhood Studies or related fields. Each intervention specialist is certified in First Aid & CPR and receives ongoing educational training on various topics of children’s development periodically.
